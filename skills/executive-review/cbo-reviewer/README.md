# CBO Reviewer

> Pressure-test the commercial side of a proposal before a real Chief Business Officer does it for you, in the meeting.

One seat of the executive-review suite. Works standalone.

## What it produces

A DRAFT Word review document, `<artifact-name>-cbo-review.docx`, saved to OneDrive at `/Documents/Cowork/reviews/`, containing:

- A verdict: PROCEED, PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS or NOT READY, with rationale.
- Three to seven findings, each anchored to a specific quote, slide, page or section of your document (no generic feedback).
- Commercial risks from the CBO mandate: deal structure, partner incentives, pricing power, concentration, reversibility, opportunity cost.
- What evidence would change the verdict.
- The five questions the real executive would ask in the meeting.

If `/Documents/Cowork/org-profile.md` exists in your OneDrive, the review uses it as company context; otherwise it runs generic and tells you where to find the template.

## Install in 60 seconds

1. Copy this folder into OneDrive at `/Documents/Cowork/skills/` (keep the folder name `cbo-reviewer` and the lowercase `skills`).
2. Start a NEW Cowork conversation: skills are only discovered when a conversation starts.
3. Use one of the trigger phrases below.

## Example triggers

- "Run a CBO review on Documents/Proposals/q3-partner-proposal.docx."
- "Review this business case as a Chief Business Officer before I take it to the exec team."
- "Pressure-test the commercial model and deal terms in this deck."
- "I pitch this partnership plan on Thursday. What would the CBO tear apart?"

## Why this exists

Most proposals meet their first hostile commercial reading inside the actual executive meeting, which is the most expensive place to learn that the deal terms are one-sided or that the demand evidence is pilot enthusiasm. This skill is the Chief Business Officer seat of the executive-review suite, built for this repository in response to recurring requests for a way to pressure-test documents before real executive reviews. The org profile template lives in this skill's references folder.
